## 1. **Purchase Amount Over Time**
This chart shows how customer spending changes over time.  
**Why it matters:**  
- Helps identify sales spikes or drops  
- Shows seasonal demand patterns  
- Helps plan marketing campaigns  

---

## 2. **Product Category Popularity**
A bar or pie chart displays how many items were purchased in each category.  
**Why it matters:**  
- Reveals best‑selling product groups  
- Helps optimize stock levels  
- Supports decisions on promotions and bundles  

---

## 3. **Customer Demographics (Age & Gender)**
Shows age distribution and gender purchasing behavior.  
**Why it matters:**  
- Understand who your primary buyers are  
- Tailor ads and promotions  
- Detect new growth segments  

---

## 4. **Purchase Frequency Analysis**
Shows how often customers buy (e.g., monthly, quarterly).  
**Why it matters:**  
- Identifies loyal vs occasional buyers  
- Supports retention strategies  
- Helps design subscription or loyalty programs  

---

## 5. **Discount & Promotion Effectiveness**
Shows how discounts impact purchase amounts and volume.  
**Why it matters:**  
- Measures if discounts increase sales  
- Avoids unnecessary margin loss  
- Helps refine promotional strategy
